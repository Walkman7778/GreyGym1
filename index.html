About the App

Gray-GYM is a mobile application designed to improve cognitive abilities, memory, and focus. We provide scientifically-backed brain training exercises through engaging gameplay mechanics.

  • Stroop Test: Enhances cognitive flexibility and reaction speed.
  • Schulte Tables: Improves peripheral vision and visual search skills.
  • Memory Sequences: Strengthens short-term and working memory.
  • Word Games: Boosts vocabulary and mental processing.

User Data & Transparency

To provide a seamless experience, Gray-GYM requests access to certain user information:

  • Google Authentication: We use your Google account to securely sign you in and protect your profile.
  • Progress Sync: We store your game scores and achievements in the cloud so you can access your progress on any device.
  • Phone Authentication (OTP): Used exclusively to verify your identity and link your game data to a secure account.

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
