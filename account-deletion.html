Gray-GYM — Account Deletion

Brain Training App  ·  greygym.app@gmail.com

This page explains how to request deletion of your Gray-GYM account and all associated data. You can delete your account directly inside the app (recommended) or by sending us an email.

Method 1 — Delete directly in the app (recommended)

Immediate · No waiting required
1
Open GreyGym and go to your Profile On the home screen, tap the Account icon (person silhouette) in the top-right corner.
2
Sign in if prompted You must be signed in to access account settings.
3
Scroll to the bottom of the Profile screen You will see two buttons: "Sign Out" and "Delete Account" (shown in red).
4
Tap "Delete Account" A confirmation dialog will appear warning you that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
5
Confirm deletion Tap "Delete" in the dialog. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted immediately.

Method 2 — Request deletion by email

Processed within 30 days

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion by email, contact us at:

📧 greygym.app@gmail.com

Please include: the email address or phone number associated with your GreyGym account. We will process your request within 30 days.

What data is deleted

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All progress, scores, and settings will be lost.
Data type Action on deletion Retention period
Firebase Authentication account (email, phone, linked providers) Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Display name Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Game statistics (scores, session count, streaks) Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Leaderboard entries (Arena results) Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Daily quest progress Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Profile photo URL (if provided via Google/Apple) Permanently deleted 0 days (immediate)
Local device data (SharedPreferences, local DB) Remains on your device Until you uninstall the app or clear app data in Android settings
Anonymized analytics data (Google Analytics for Firebase) May be retained in aggregated, non-identifiable form Up to 14 months (per Google Analytics policy)
